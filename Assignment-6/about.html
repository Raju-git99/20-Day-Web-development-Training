About Ideal Institute of Engineering

Ideal Institute of Engineering, established in 2010, is approved by AICTE and affiliated to MAKAUT (formerly WBUT). The institution is committed to producing globally competent engineers by promoting a culture of innovation, technology, and research.

We aim to develop ethical, responsible engineers who contribute positively to society. The campus provides a serene environment, modern labs, and dedicated faculty to ensure holistic development.

Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in engineering education and research that fosters innovation and entrepreneurship.

Mission

  • Provide quality technical education with a focus on professional ethics and lifelong learning.
  • Encourage research, creativity, and innovation among students and faculty.
  • Strengthen industry-academia collaboration to meet global challenges.
